About Me



Adil Soomro is a junior at Marlboro High School. He currently Volunteers for the Marlboro First Aid Squad and holds a position of Cadet Lieutenant. Adil has been on over 40 calls with patient care. He has successfully completed the EMT Initial Hybrid Program - NJOEMS Course #147981 on December 4, 2022 at the Marlboro First Aid Squad. After 220 hours of class time and a national test, Adil was certified with the National Registry of Emergency Medical Technicians as an EMT and an EMT recognized by teh state of New Jersey. Additionally, Adil holds a strong passion for photography as well as web designing. He is currrently enrolled in the Web Design Pathway Program at Marlboro High School and has learned HTML, CSS and started JavaScript.
